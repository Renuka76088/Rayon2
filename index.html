About Parekh Rayon

Parekh Rayon is a premium apparel brand that blends the timeless elegance of Indian fashion with a contemporary global aesthetic. Since 2007 our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to quality and cutting-edge style.

What started in the heart of RAIPUR, CG has grown into a pan-India movement. Every garment we craft balances traditional craftsmanship with modern trends, built on sustainable sourcing and ethical production.

"Where comfort meets fashion, and style meets value."
Our Vision

To transform India into the global capital of sustainable rayon fashion—where high-quality, stylish apparel is accessible to everyone who values elegance.

Our Mission
  • Finest quality rayon fabrics
  • Trend-setting affordable collections
  • 100% Customer satisfaction
  • Eco-friendly & ethical practices
